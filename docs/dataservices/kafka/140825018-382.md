# 3.8.2

kafka-cluster-entity-operator、kafka-cluster-kafka-exporter pod未绑定指定节点

## Cause
- 未配置节点亲和性规则

## Resolution
- 在entityoperator和kafkaexporter配置段添加nodeAffinity配置:
entityOperator:
  template:
    pod:
      affinity:
        nodeAffinity:
kafkaExporter:
  template:
    pod:
      affinity:
        nodeAffinity:

## [workaround]
